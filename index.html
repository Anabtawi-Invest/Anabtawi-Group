About us

A Jordanian story built on quality

Anabtawi Sweets was founded in 1983 with a simple promise: prepare authentic sweets with excellent ingredients and genuine hospitality.

Over the following four decades, the company grew into a broad branch network while preserving the recipes, skill, and attention to detail that shaped its reputation.

Growing together

Close to our customers

Our locations serve communities throughout Jordan and Palestine, supported by teams who share the same standards of freshness, service, and care.
